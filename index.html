About Me

I am a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca, with a Bachelor’s degree in Telecommunications Systems and Technologies, and I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity at Transilvania University of Brașov. My academic background combines engineering fundamentals with a growing focus on secure and reliable software systems.
